Lasagna

Description

A really tasty lasagna recipe.

Ingredients

  • Meat

  • Onion and garlic

  • Tomato

  • Sugar

  • Spices and sseasoning

  • Lasagna noodles

  • Cheese

  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.

  2. Cook the noodles.

  3. Make the ricotta mixture.

  4. Layer the lasagna accordingly the recipe instructions.

  5. Cover with foil and bake.

  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
